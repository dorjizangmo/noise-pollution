Welcome – Let's Explore the Issue of Noise Pollution

Noise pollution is a growing concern with significant health and environmental impacts. It's more than just an annoyance; it can lead to hearing loss, cardiovascular problems, sleep disturbances, and even affect mental health.

Causes

  • Traffic
  • Construction
  • Industries

Effects

Hearing loss, sleep disturbance, and lower property values.

Solutions

Use earplugs, plant trees, and encourage public transportation.

How to Reduce Noise Pollution

  1. Use ear protection in noisy areas
  2. Enforce noise regulations
  3. Promote green spaces and noise barriers
